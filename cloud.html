CLOUD SERVICES

Cloud services is the comprehensive practice of protecting computer systems, networks, devices, programs, and data from digital attacks, damage, or unauthorized access. Its core purpose is to ensure the confidentiality, integrity, and availability (CIA triad) of information in our increasingly connected world. Key Components An effective cybersecurity strategy relies on a combination of people, processes, and technology. People: Users must be educated to understand and comply with basic security principles, such as creating strong passwords, identifying phishing attempts, and handling sensitive information safely. Processes: Organizations need a framework for responding to both attempted and successful cyberattacks, including incident response plans, risk management protocols, and disaster recovery procedures to ensure business continuity. Technology: Various security tools are implemented to defend digital assets, including firewalls, antivirus software, encryption, and intrusion detection systems. .
